THE PROBLEM

Investors Don't Lack Deals. They Lack Verified Assets.

Across private markets, assets are presented through pitch decks, data rooms, and investment memoranda.

Yet basic questions often remain unanswered:

  • ⚠ Which entity legally holds it?

InvestPASS introduces a structured verification layer designed to improve transparency around asset identity and ownership.
